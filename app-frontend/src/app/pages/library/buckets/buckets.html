What is a bucket?

A bucket is a personal collection of scenes from many different sources. Think of it as a bookmark folder of images.

Are my buckets public?

Your buckets can only be seen by you.

How many buckets can I have?

You have an unlimited number of buckets.

Do buckets have size limits?

Nope. You can bookmark as many scenes as you need.
